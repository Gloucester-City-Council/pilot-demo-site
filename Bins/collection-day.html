Putting your bins out

To make sure your bins are collected:

  • Put bins out by 7am on your collection day (the evening before is best)
  • Place at the boundary of your property ‚Äî where your drive meets the pavement, or at your garden gate
  • Make sure the crew can reach them ‚Äî they cannot enter your property or open gates
  • Lids must close fully ‚Äî if your bin is overflowing, it may not be collected
  • Bring bins back in within 24 hours of collection

What if my bin isn't collected?

If your bin isn't collected by 6pm on your collection day:

  1. Check the crew didn't refuse it ‚Äî was the lid closed? Was it in the right place?
  2. Check for service updates ‚Äî bad weather or vehicle breakdowns can delay collections
  3. Wait until the next working day ‚Äî crews sometimes run late
  4. If still not collected after 2 working days, report a missed collection

We aim to return within 2 working days to collect missed bins.

Bank holidays and Christmas

Your collection day changes when there's a bank holiday:

  • Bank holiday Monday ‚Äî collections delayed by one day all week
  • Friday collections ‚Äî may be collected on Saturday instead
  • Christmas and New Year ‚Äî check our Christmas collection dates

We'll post updates on this page and on social media before bank holidays.
