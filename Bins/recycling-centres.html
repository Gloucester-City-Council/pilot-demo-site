Household Recycling Centres (HRCs), sometimes called 'tips' or 'the dump', are for items that don't fit in your bins. All centres require you to book before visiting.

What you can take to recycling centres

Household Recycling Centres accept a wide range of materials and items including:

Garden waste
Cardboard and paper
Glass bottles and jars
Plastic bottles and containers
Metal cans and scrap metal
Wood and timber
Electrical items
Batteries
Textiles and clothing
Books and media
Household furniture
Mattresses
Fridges and freezers
Paint and chemicals
Rubble and soil (small amounts)
Tyres (limited quantities)

üí° Tip: For a complete list of what can and cannot be taken, and any restrictions or charges, visit www.gloucestershirerecycles.com

Important information

üöó Van permits

If you're visiting in a van, pick-up truck, or large vehicle, you may need a permit. Check the Gloucestershire Recycles website for details.

üè† Household waste only

Recycling centres are for household waste only. Business and commercial waste is not accepted.

üìã What to bring

Bring proof of Gloucestershire residency (such as a utility bill or Council Tax letter) and your booking confirmation.

‚è∞ Allow enough time

Booking slots are typically 15 minutes. Make sure to arrive on time and allow enough time to unload your vehicle safely.
