Before you report a missed collection

Only report if:

  • It's past 6pm on your collection day (or the next working day if it was a bank holiday)
  • You put your bin out by 7am on collection day
  • Your bin was at the edge of your property (where the crew could reach it)
  • The lid was closed and nothing was sticking out
  • You didn't have wrong items in your bin (this causes it to be refused)
Collection still in progress?

Crews work from 7am to 6pm. If it's before 6pm on your collection day, your bin may still be collected today. Please wait until after 6pm before reporting.

What if your bin was refused?

Sometimes bins aren't collected because:

  • Wrong items inside ‚Äî check what goes in which bin
  • Lid wouldn't close ‚Äî bin was too full or items were sticking out
  • Bin wasn't accessible ‚Äî behind a locked gate or too far from the road
  • Too heavy to lift ‚Äî crew couldn't safely lift it

If your bin was refused, you'll usually see a note or sticker explaining why. Remove the problem items and put your bin out on your next collection day.

How to prevent missed collections

  • Put bins out the night before ‚Äî don't wait until the morning
  • Place at the edge of your property ‚Äî where your drive meets the pavement
  • Make sure the lid closes ‚Äî don't overfill or force items in
  • Keep it accessible ‚Äî crew can't open gates or enter property
  • Use the right bin ‚Äî wrong items cause bins to be refused
  • Set up reminders ‚Äî get email or SMS reminders

Response time

Within 2 working days

We aim to return within 2 working days of receiving your report. Most collections are completed within 24 hours.
