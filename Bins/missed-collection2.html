Before you report a missed collection

You can report a missed collection if:

  • Your bin was due for collection today or yesterday
  • It's before 4pm on the day after your collection
  • You put your bin out by 7am on collection day
  • Your bin was at the edge of your property (where the crew could reach it)
  • The lid was closed and nothing was sticking out
  • You didn't have wrong items in your bin (this causes it to be refused)
‚è∞ Reporting deadline: 4pm the next business day

Crews update the system as they complete rounds. You can report a missed collection until 4pm the day after your scheduled collection. After that, you'll need to wait for your next scheduled collection.
