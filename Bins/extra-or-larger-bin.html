You can get an extra or larger black bin if you're recycling everything you can by making full use of the recycling containers and if any of the following apply to you.

Who is eligible

If you're having problems managing your waste and don't meet the criteria, please ensure you are making full use of the weekly recycling service.

For us to assess your application you must be recycling your waste by having a minimum of:

  • 2 recycling boxes
  • 1 blue sack
  • 1 large food caddy
  • 1 small food caddy

Use our order recycling container service to order boxes, lids, sacks and food caddies.
