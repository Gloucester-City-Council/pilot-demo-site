üè¢ Flats & Shared Properties

If you live in a flat or apartment, you'll likely have communal bins instead of individual containers.

What you'll have

  • Communal bins: Shared recycling and waste bins in a designated bin store
  • Food caddy: Each flat gets a 7L kitchen caddy for food waste
  • Communal food bin: A brown 140L bin in the bin store for all residents

Collection schedule for flats

Food waste: Weekly collection
Recycling and general waste: Fortnightly on alternating weeks

Bin sizes for flats

The number and size of bins depends on how many flats share them:

  • For every 8 flats: One 1100L communal bin
  • Recycling bins: 240L or 360L depending on the development

Problems with communal bins?

If your communal bins are:

  • Always full or overflowing
  • Damaged or need replacing
  • Not being collected properly

Call us: 01452 396 396

Information for developers, builders, and residents moving into new build properties.

When will new properties get bins?

Before bins can be delivered to new residential properties:

  • The property must be registered for Council Tax
  • The developer must request a site visit from our contractor
  • The contractor needs to agree that collections can be carried out safely

For new residents in completed developments

If you've just moved into a new build property and don't have bins yet, order your recycling containers online and contact us about your black bin.

üìã Planning & storage guidance for developers

‚ñº
Bin store requirements

Developers must consider bin storage as part of planning submissions:

  • Collections can only be made from designated bin stores with adequate access
  • Under Building Regulations (2010) Part H: Householders must not carry waste containers further than 30m
  • Bin stores must be accessible for collection vehicles
Storage space calculations

For individual houses:

  • 1 x 180L black bin (collected fortnightly)
  • 2 x green boxes for recycling
  • 1 x blue sack
  • 1 x outdoor food caddy

For flats/multiple occupancy:

  • 1 x 1100L bin per 8 flats
  • 240L or 360L recycling bins as appropriate
  • 1 x 140L communal food waste bin per bin store
Contact for developers

For full waste storage and collection guidance, download the Waste & Recycling Storage Guidance (PDF)
